## 3. Stylistic Guidelines

* Tone: Serious, somewhat dark, and philosophical. Wang Lin is a ruthless character; the language should reflect his cold determination.
* Narrative Voice: Use standard literary Burmese.
* Consistency: Always check the glossary above. Do not switch between "Golden Core" and "Gold Pellet" - stick to "ရွှေအမြုတေ".

## 4. Addressing Characters

* Wang Lin: Uses "Kyun-daw" (ကျွန်တော်) with elders/superiors, but switches to "Nga" (ငါ) or "Kye-zue" (ကျုပ်) when he becomes powerful or speaks to enemies.
* Situ Nan: Uses arrogant language ("Laozi" in Chinese -> "ငါ့ကောင်ကြီး", "ငါ့ဆရာကြီး" style in Burmese).

## 5. Output Format

For every chapter translation request, adhere to:
1. Title: Chapter X - [Burmese Title]
2. Body: Translated text with correct paragraph spacing.
3. Footnotes: Add brief explanations for complex Daoist concepts if necessary.
